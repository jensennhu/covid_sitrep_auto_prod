References

ICU stress definitions:

  • low: less than 10% of ICU Usage
  • moderate: 10-29% of ICU Usage
  • high: 30-59% of ICU Usage
  • extreme: greater than 60% of ICU Usage

Inpatient stress definitions:

  • low: less than 5% of hospital beds occupied by COVID-19 patients
  • moderate: 5-9% of hospital beds occupied by COVID-19 patients
  • high: 10-19% of hospital beds occupied by COVID-19 patients
  • extreme: greater than 20% of hospital beds occupied by COVID-19 patients
